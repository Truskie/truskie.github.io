Meet the Team

My name is Terryl Neal. I am from Placencia Village and have a keen interest in Information Technology. I attended Independence High School and graduated with a major in Tourism. I have also attended Independence Junior College and did most of an Associates Degree in Business. I had to drop that major due to COVID-19, unfortunately, and successfully completed an Associates in Information Technology at IJC. I am currently attending UB to get my Bachelor's Degree in IT as it has become my passion. I tend to tinker with hardware more than software but I have experience working with both and learn fairly quickly for IT related work as my passion is high for the field. I do not support the use of AI for most workloads as it is wholly unnecessary and tends to lead to more bug-fixing than actual work, and I believe that sucks the fun out of it. However I do agree with its limited use as a tool for learning and light editing of works. I firmly believe that humans should be the ones doing the job mostly as it leads to less errors by having one good coder over multiple vibe-coders.
